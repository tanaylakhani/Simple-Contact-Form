== Installation ==

**Installation Instruction**

* Unpack the *.zip file and extract the /simple-contact-form/ folder.
* Drop the 'simple-contact-form' folder into your 'wp-content/plugins' folder.
* In word press administration panels, click on plug-in from the menu tab.
* You should see your new 'simple contact form' plug-in listed under Inactive plug-in tab.
* To turn the word presses plug-in on, click activate.
* Go to widget link under Appearance tab, Drag & drop 'simple contact form' widget to your desired location in the active sidebar.
* Click on the configure button (small down triangle) for the 'simple contact form' widget and here you can customize all the widget front end styles.
